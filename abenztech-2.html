Anthony the Analyst

Anthony Benders is a highly skilled and experienced data analyst with a passion for using data to tell stories and help people make better decisions. He has over 10 years of experience in customer service and is proficient in a variety of data analysis tools, including SQL, Tableau, and Python. He is also a team player and a quick learner.

Benders is passionate about using data to solve problems and improve business performance. He is also a skilled communicator who is able to clearly and concisely communicate his findings to both technical and non-technical audiences.

In his previous role at AVTECH Institute of Technology, Benders used his skills to generate a data set from a relational database using SQL and import the data set into Excel and Tableau to create interactive dashboards. The dashboards were used to track and analyze data for businesses, and it helped the businesses to make better decisions.

Benders is eager to use his skills and experience to help a new company achieve its goals. He is confident that he can use his passion for data, his ability to communicate effectively, and his team player skills to make a significant contribution to any organization.
